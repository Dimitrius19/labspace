# T-Life Capital — Vertical B2B SaaS Sourcing Brief

Cluster: under-served Greek + EU SME verticals where software is bad/absent, vertical SaaS is weak or US-only, and AI-native build leapfrogs without retrofit. Four candidates clear the filters. Restaurants and home-services trades dropped at filter 1: Toast/Lightspeed and ServiceTitan/Jobber shipped AI agents + EU expansion in 2024–2026.

---

## 1. Boatyard & Yacht-Management OS — AI-native ops backbone for small Greek/Med yards and fleet managers

**Buyer set (named):** ~180 named Greek boatyards/marinas in Attica, Cyclades, Ionian and Dodecanese with 30–300 wet/dry berths each (reachable via HEMEXPO and the Hellenic Marinas Association); ~120 sub-50-yacht charter/management firms in Greece + Croatia + Balearics (reachable via Greek Yacht Brokers Association, MYBA, ECPY). Secondary: ~400 EU equivalents in Italy, Spain, Croatia, France.

**Why AI-native wins on unit cost:** A 60-yacht manager runs 4–6 ops coordinators on WhatsApp threads with captains, parts orders, surveyor scheduling, marina invoicing in 3 languages, charter handover, insurance/flag-state paperwork. One agent stack (multilingual intake, parts RAG, supplier voice/email negotiation, OCR for handwritten work orders) compresses to 1 human + agents — ~8× labor compression.

**Why incumbents can't ship this in 12 months:** ServiceTitan/Jobber are home-services-shaped. Marine has tide windows, captain-as-end-user, multi-flag regulation, parts shipped by boat, and trilingual Greek/English/Italian norms. Nautilus Labs/Helm CONNECT serve commercial shipping >40m. No serious EU SMB-marine SaaS exists.

**Why a Greek/EU sovereign cannot supply this free:** Greek Ministry of Maritime focuses on flag-state e-filing (already free via e-DLA). Operational coordination across private yards, suppliers and captains is not a public-good scope — no agency builds CRM-grade ops software.

**Structural defensibility:** (a) Two-sided liquidity: yards + parts suppliers + surveyors form a directory that compounds; (b) Greek-language marine corpus + handwritten work-order dataset; (c) Family relationships in Greek maritime/industrial network through Tryfon family connections; (d) Insurance-broker integration (Lloyd's coverholders) creates switching cost.

**T-Life asset edge:** Greek family principal opens HEMEXPO/Posidonia doors; Greek-language data advantage; family balance sheet can hold receivables during seasonal cash gaps (real moat in marine — banks won't).

**Phase 0 kill test (binary, ≤€50K, ≤16 weeks):** Sign 6 paid LOIs at €600+/berth/yr or €40/yacht/mo from 3 Attica yards + 3 charter managers, conditional on a working WhatsApp-to-work-order agent demo on their real backlog. Binary: 6 signed = proceed, ≤3 = kill.

**Honest p(survives 2-round red-team):** 0.45 — main risk is TAM ceiling (<€80M ARR in EU SMB marine); secondary is seasonality concentrating churn signal.

## 2. Greek/EU Accountant-in-the-Loop Bookkeeping OS — AI agent that does the books, licensed accountant signs

**Buyer set (named):** ~14,000 Greek licensed accounting offices (logistika grafeia) serving 5–80 SME clients each; primary wedge = the ~600 mid-tier offices (10–40 staff) in Athens/Thessaloniki/Patras reachable via the Economic Chamber of Greece (OEE) and PanHellenic Federation of Accountants (POFEE). EU expansion via Italian commercialisti (~118K) and Spanish asesorías.

**Why AI-native wins on unit cost:** A junior bookkeeper at €1,400/mo processes ~80 SME files. An agent that ingests myDATA e-invoices, classifies against Greek chart-of-accounts, reconciles bank lines, drafts VAT returns and flags exceptions handles 400+ files per junior — 5× compression. The accountant of record (legally required signer) keeps liability and client; we sell to them, not around them.

**Why incumbents can't ship this in 12 months:** SoftOne/EpsilonNet/Entersoft are ERP record-keepers with bolt-on AI chat — not autonomous ledger agents. Their per-seat model means an agent replacing 4 of 5 seats is cannibalistic; they won't ship it. Pennylane (FR) is closest EU peer — doesn't address myDATA or Greek tax calendar.

**Why a Greek/EU sovereign cannot supply this free:** AADE (Greek tax authority) already provides myDATA + free e-invoicing portal; that's the **input**, not the workflow. The accountant's bookkeeping workflow is a private professional service — no agency builds it.

**Structural defensibility:** (a) Direct myDATA connector + 3-year reconciled Greek SMB transaction corpus = data moat for accuracy benchmarking; (b) accountant-of-record contracts = regulatory relationship; (c) revenue-share with accounting office aligns incumbent channel against Greek ERP vendors; (d) liability-shifted architecture (accountant signs) gives us E&O risk profile competitors can't match without years of claims data.

**T-Life asset edge:** 4-generation family pharma business = direct credibility with mid-market Greek accountants who already serve pharma supply chain; family network can introduce first 30 offices.

**Phase 0 kill test (≤€50K, ≤16 weeks):** Recruit 5 accounting offices to run agent in shadow mode on 50 real SME files for one VAT quarter; pass criterion = ≥92% line-classification accuracy and ≥3 of 5 offices sign €X/file/mo contract. Kill if ≤2 sign.

**Honest p(survives 2-round red-team):** 0.50 — main risk is SoftOne reaction (they have 2026 AI roadmap); mitigant is channel — accountants, not end-SMEs, are the buyer.

## 3. Short-Let Property Co-Host OS — AI agent stack for the boutique Greek property-manager (the "Mr. Yorgos with 40 keys")

**Buyer set (named):** ~2,400 named small Greek short-let co-host operators with 20–200 keys (concentrated Athens, Mykonos, Santorini, Paros, Chania, Rhodes, Thessaloniki; reachable via STAMA Greece short-term-rental association and listing-channel partner programs). EU mirror: ~8,000 similar operators across Spain (Andalusia, Balearics), Portugal, Italy, Croatia.

**Why AI-native wins on unit cost:** A 60-key co-host runs 3–4 staff on guest messaging in 5 languages, cleaning dispatch, pricing, AADE filings, damage claims, review-recovery. Autonomous agents for guest comms + cleaner-routing + AADE + review-response collapses this to 1 ops human per 200 keys — ~6× labor compression on a function where labor is 60% of co-host margin.

**Why incumbents can't ship this in 12 months:** Hostaway/Guesty/Lodgify are channel-manager+PMS shapes with AI guest-message **drafting** (human send). They won't ship autonomous send/refund authority because their enterprise (1000+ key) customers reject auto-action. The wedge is the 20–200-key segment that wants automation but can't afford Guesty enterprise. Mews/Cloudbeds are hotel-PMS — wrong shape.

**Why a Greek/EU sovereign cannot supply this free:** AADE short-let registry is a filing endpoint, free. Operational co-hosting is private commerce, not a public good.

**Structural defensibility:** (a) Direct Airbnb/Booking/VRBO partner-API integrations (gated, 12+ months); (b) AADE filing + Greek tax-edge corpus; (c) multilingual guest-comms data; (d) cleaner/handyman supply graph per island that compounds — a new entrant in Mykonos must rebuild it. Family bridge financing for co-hosts in shoulder season is a real wedge.

**T-Life asset edge:** Family real-estate footprint in Greece (typical for 4-gen Greek industrial family) — first 5 customers in-house or one degree out. Capital to underwrite co-host receivables.

**Phase 0 kill test (≤€50K, ≤16 weeks):** Pilot with 8 co-hosts (total ~600 keys) on autonomous guest-comms + AADE filing for one full month; pass criterion = ≥90% messages sent without human edit AND ≥4 sign annual contracts at €6–10/key/mo. Kill if ≤2 sign.

**Honest p(survives 2-round red-team):** 0.40 — main risk is Airbnb itself shipping a "co-host AI" in 2026 (they announced co-host marketplace 2024). Mitigant: Airbnb won't touch AADE, won't dispatch cleaners, won't underwrite cash.

## 4. Veterinary Clinic OS for Greek + Southern-EU independents — AI agent for triage, records, and pet-owner comms

**Buyer set (named):** ~1,900 independent Greek vet clinics (Hellenic Veterinary Medical Society, HVMS) + ~3,200 in Portugal/Spain/Italy independent (non-IVC/non-Mars-consolidated). Wedge = 1–3 vet clinics doing >€300K revenue, no SOR beyond paper + Excel + WhatsApp.

**Why AI-native wins on unit cost:** Solo vet runs 1–2 receptionists on phone triage, booking, vaccine-reminder SMS, insurance pre-auth, dictation. An agent stack (Greek/Spanish/Italian voice intake, vet-supervised triage, dictation-to-record, vaccine recall) replaces 1 of 2 reception FTEs — €18–24K/yr saved vs €2.4K/yr subscription. Marginal cost per call <€0.10.

**Why incumbents can't ship this in 12 months:** Provet Cloud/IDEXX Animana/VetIT are records-shaped, chain-focused (IVC, VetPartners, Mars). They're not building Greek-language voice agents for solos. Vetstoria/PetDesk don't speak Greek. Consolidators actively avoid building tooling that strengthens independents.

**Why a Greek/EU sovereign cannot supply this free:** No public-good scope — vet care is private practice. EU pet-passport and rabies-traceability is a filing layer, not workflow.

**Structural defensibility:** (a) multilingual vet symptom-triage dataset reviewed by licensed vets = years to replicate; (b) integrations with national pet-microchip registries and pet-insurance carriers (Petalia, Hellas Direct) create data-flow moat; (c) PE consolidation threat means independents pay for tooling that lets them resist or sell at higher multiples.

**T-Life asset edge:** Pharma-family medical credibility; family pharma distribution may already serve veterinary channel (animal pharma is adjacent); same buyer psychology as pharmacist.

**Phase 0 kill test (≤€50K, ≤16 weeks):** Deploy voice-triage + reminder agent at 5 Athens/Thessaloniki clinics for 12 weeks; pass = ≥40% of inbound calls handled without human, AND ≥3 of 5 sign €200/mo annual contract. Kill if ≤2 sign or call-handling <25%.

**Honest p(survives 2-round red-team):** 0.45 — main risk is PE consolidators (IVC, VetPartners) accelerating roll-up in Greece/Iberia and eliminating independents we sell to; mitigant is that consolidation drives the remaining independents toward exactly this tooling.
