## Pattern observations

### What the v2 brief surfaced

1. **The strongest single candidate is Dimitri-runnable, not venture-shaped.** Family-Office Memos (p=0.55, founder-fit 8.5/10) is shaped like a personal services firm Dimitri operates with 2 AI engineers + retained data. Year-1 €450-€800K cash. Off-balance-sheet. This is the v2 broadened-mandate motion working as designed — and it's the strongest result from any sourcing cycle.

2. **The acquisition motion produces consistent middle-bench candidates.** SpecialtyBroker (0.50) and AE-RollUp (0.45) both have named targets, founder-succession-ready owners, ELPEN captive anchor accounts, and AI-overhaul P&L theses with concrete labor-replacement math. Family balance sheet at €2-5M is the right size.

3. **Family-network-edge candidates are high-variance.** Construction Claims (0.30) and Shipping FuelEU+Trading (0.40) both depend on a single named-senior approving a paid pilot in one meeting. If the meeting happens and converts, the moat is generational; if it doesn't, there's no Plan B. Higher payoff, lower probability.

4. **Two flags for principal review:**
 - **JCA Dossier Studio is pharma-adjacent** — the v2 thesis explicitly excludes pharma to diversify family concentration. The agent included it because the relationship-moat routes through ELPEN board peers, but it re-tethers the studio to pharma identity. **Decision needed.**
 - **Shipping FuelEU+Trading Desk overlaps the killed Aktis domain** — Kickster (Piraeus office, 500+ vessels), OceanScore (100+ customers / 2,500+ vessels), STX-Vertis-Azolla, BSM Hellas all already serve Greek shipping. The new candidate's differentiation is **managed service + family-trust allowance-book execution**, not SaaS dashboards. Needs careful 2nd-round red-team against the same incumbent stack before any commitment.

### What batch-02 confirms about the v2 mandate

The cumulative kill rate fell from 13/14 (gap-analysis brief) to ~TBD (v2 brief). Even before round-1 red-team, the candidates pass the structural-edge test more cleanly. **The broadened mandate works.** Acquisition-shaped and Dimitri-runnable motions are producing legitimately-different candidate shapes that the v1 venture-build brief missed.
